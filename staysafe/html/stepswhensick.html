IF YOU HAVE OR THINK YOU ARE INFECTED WITH CORONAVIRUS MAKE SURE TO DO THINGS MENTION BELOW

01

STAY HOME EXCEPT TO GET MEDICAL CARE

MOST PEOPLE WITH COVID-19 CNA RECOVER AT HOME WITHOUT MEDICAL CARE. STAY HYDRATED AND MAKE SURE NOT TO GET IN TOUCH WITH ANYONE.

02

SEPARATE YOURSELF FORM OTHERS

As much as possible stay in a specific room. Preferably use your own kitchenware. Distance yourself as much as possible

03

Call ahead before visiting your doctor

Call your doctor beforehand to prevent mishappening also you can contact the government helpline from the link in our website

04

COVER WHILE SNEEZING

Make sure to cover your mouth andnose with a tissue when you cough or sneeze as coronavirus spreads very rapidly with air.

05

SYMPTOM MONITORING

Make sure to continuosly monitor your symptoms and make sure to hospitalized if your symptom got worse

06

CLEANING AND DISINFECTING SURFACES

Make sure to thoroughly clean the surfaces that you are prone to touch to prevent further transmission.
